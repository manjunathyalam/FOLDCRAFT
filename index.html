Three real steps, not a sales pitch

Render

Your text becomes a grid

Each page in your book becomes one column of a black-and-white grid. The taller your book, the more vertical detail survives.

Map

Pixels become millimetres

FoldCraft scales the grid to your actual page height, so every mark corresponds to a real, measurable point on a real page.

Mark

Every page gets fold lines

You get a per-page list: where to fold, and which way — ready to print or read straight off the screen while you work.

Build your pattern
